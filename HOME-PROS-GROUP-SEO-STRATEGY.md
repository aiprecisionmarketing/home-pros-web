## 1. Executive Summary

Home Pros Group is a family-owned furnace, duct, and dryer vent cleaning company based in Stony Plain, AB, serving Spruce Grove, Parkland County, and the Greater Edmonton area. The business has strong fundamentals: 20+ years of experience, 10,000+ clients served, a 4.9-star Google rating with 200+ reviews, transparent flat-rate pricing ($345 all-in), and an owner-operated model (Eric and Keith on every job).

**The problem:** The website is severely underperforming for SEO. The most recent SEO health report scored the site at 32/100 (Critical), with a Technical score of 0, Crawlability at 92, and Content at 13. There are duplicate page titles, missing meta descriptions, thin content pages, broken links, and no blog content publishing cadence. The homepage title tag -- "Furnace Cleaning,Duct Cleaning,Furnace Repair,Edmonton" -- is poorly optimized and misleading (HPG does not do furnace repair, and the primary service area is Spruce Grove/Stony Plain, not Edmonton).

**The opportunity:** The Spruce Grove/Stony Plain/Parkland County market has limited competition for long-tail HVAC cleaning keywords. Most competitors are Edmonton-based companies that treat these areas as secondary. Home Pros Group has the unique advantage of being locally based with a physical address in Stony Plain. Combined with the existing review profile, flat-rate pricing differentiator, and property manager segment, there is a clear path to dominating local search.

**Applying Hormozi's frameworks:** The $100M Playbooks provide the strategic backbone for this plan -- specifically the Proof Checklist (overwhelming proof beats all marketing), Lead Nurture (speed and availability), Pricing Playbook (price is the biggest profit lever), Marketing Machine (systematize proof capture), and Fast Cash Playbook (promotions to existing customers). Every recommendation below ties back to these principles.

## 7. Technical SEO Improvements

### Priority 1: Critical Fixes (Week 1-2)

1. **Fix the homepage title tag.** Change from "Furnace Cleaning,Duct Cleaning,Furnace Repair,Edmonton" to:
   - "Furnace & Duct Cleaning Spruce Grove & Stony Plain | Home Pros Group"
   - Remove "Furnace Repair" -- HPG does not do repairs. This is misleading and could hurt trust.
   - Remove "Edmonton" from the primary title -- the business is in Stony Plain. Edmonton can be mentioned in meta descriptions and service area pages.

2. **Fix all duplicate title tags.** Every page needs a unique, keyword-optimized title. Format: "[Primary Keyword] [Location] | Home Pros Group"

3. **Add meta descriptions to ALL pages.** Every page needs a unique 150-155 character meta description with primary keyword, location, and CTA.

4. **Fix the broken link** on /category/reviews/ (the email protection 404).

5. **Add LocalBusiness schema** (JSON-LD) to every page:
   ```json
   {
     "@context": "https://schema.org",
     "@type": "LocalBusiness",
     "name": "Home Pros Group",
     "address": {
       "@type": "PostalAddress",
       "streetAddress": "53 Umbach Road",
       "addressLocality": "Stony Plain",
       "addressRegion": "AB",
       "postalCode": "T7Z 1G1",
       "addressCountry": "CA"
     },
     "telephone": "+1-825-435-9977",
     "url": "https://homeprosgroup.com",
     "priceRange": "$345",
     "aggregateRating": {
       "@type": "AggregateRating",
       "ratingValue": "4.9",
       "reviewCount": "200"
     }
   }
   ```

6. **Consolidate phone numbers.** Pick ONE primary number and use it consistently across the entire site. Recommendation: (825) 435-9977 (newer, already on the Spruce Grove site). Add the old (780) 932-7337 as a secondary/redirect.

### Priority 2: Performance (Week 2-4)

7. **Audit and fix Core Web Vitals.** The Lighthouse score of 0 indicates severe performance issues:
   - Reduce JavaScript bloat from Elementor Pro, jQuery, and Bootstrap
   - Implement proper image optimization (lazy loading exists, but source images may be too large)
   - Enable server-side caching (page cache, browser cache headers)
   - Consider a CDN (Cloudflare) for static assets
   - Minify CSS/JS

8. **Resolve the sprucegrovefurnacecleaning.com dual-domain issue.** Two options:
   - Option A (recommended): 301 redirect sprucegrovefurnacecleaning.com to homeprosgroup.com and consolidate all content under one domain. This prevents keyword cannibalization and concentrates domain authority.
   - Option B: Keep both but make sprucegrovefurnacecleaning.com a focused landing page with canonical tags pointing to homeprosgroup.com.

9. **Implement proper image alt text.** Replace generic "Furnace Cleaning" with descriptive text: "Home Pros Group technician cleaning furnace ducts in a Spruce Grove home"

10. **Create and submit an XML sitemap** via Google Search Console. Ensure all service pages, location pages, and blog posts are included.

### Priority 3: Content & Structure (Week 3-6)

11. **Build the location-specific service pages** (listed in Section 3). Each page needs 800+ words of unique content -- NOT duplicate copy with city names swapped.

12. **Expand thin content pages** to 800+ words minimum. The 67-word and 234-word pages need substantial content additions.

13. **Implement a proper internal linking structure:**
    - Homepage links to all service category pages
    - Service category pages link to location-specific pages
    - Blog posts link to relevant service pages (and vice versa)
    - Create a "Related Services" section at the bottom of every page

14. **Add breadcrumb navigation** with BreadcrumbList schema. Current breadcrumb schema has only one item (Home Pros Group) -- needs full path.

15. **Start publishing blog content weekly.** The blog writer agent has articles ready. These need to go live on the site with proper SEO formatting.

### Priority 4: Conversion Optimization (Week 4-8)

16. **Add click-to-call buttons** on every page (especially mobile). The phone number should be a tappable link.

17. **Add an "Online Booking" option.** Per Hormozi's Lead Nurture: have inbound, outbound, AND self-scheduling options. More ways to book = more bookings.

18. **Add a sticky header/footer CTA** on mobile: "Call Now: (825) 435-9977" or "Book Online"

19. **Add exit-intent popup** with lead magnet offer (the Pricing Calculator or Per-Vent Trap guide).
